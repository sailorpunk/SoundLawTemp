Personal Injury

If you or a loved one has been injured through the wrongful act of another, chances are you are entitled to a recovery. At Donaldson & English, one of our skilled attorneys will provide you with an overview of the recovery process. We can quickly assess whether or not you may be entitled to monetary damages and the relative strengths and weaknesses of your case.

We are associated with a number of different law firms that specialize in specific types of personal injury law. As such, our expansive resources enable us and our affiliates to provide exemplary representation and to obtain the maximum amount of damages. No matter the type of accident – whether a car collision, slip and fall, or medical malpractice – chances are we have the resources to effectively advocate for your rights and obtain a recovery that you are entitled to.

    The following types of damages are what Washington law allows victims to recover:

  • Present and future medical bills
  • Physical therapy
  • Lost past and future wages
  • Pain and suffering
  • Damaged personal property
  • Household care
  • Emotional distress

Many insurance companies will attempt you to sign waivers or coerce you into making detrimental statements. We recommend you do not speak with anyone regarding your injuries besides your doctor and attorney.
